Dr. Ajay Chowbe

Principal | Educationist | Academic Leader

About Me

Dr. Ajay Chowbe is a distinguished educationist, visionary school leader, and academic administrator with over 28 years of rich experience in CBSE and ICSE schools across India. Known for his strategic foresight, disciplined governance, and people-centric leadership, he has consistently led institutions toward academic excellence and holistic development.

He currently serves as the Principal of Titans’ Public School, Amravati, bringing more than 17 years of leadership experience as a Principal and extensive teaching experience as a Senior Secondary Mathematics educator.

Educational Philosophy & Leadership Vision

Dr. Chowbe believes that education must nurture intellectual ability, moral character, and life skills simultaneously. His leadership approach aligns strongly with the objectives of the National Education Policy (NEP), emphasizing learner-centric pedagogy, teacher empowerment, and future-ready skills.
